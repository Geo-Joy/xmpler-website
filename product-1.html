TELCO DATA & ANALYTICS


  • INTRODUCTION
  • TELCO DATA & ANALYTICS
  • SALIENT FEATURES

The Telco industry generates and stores a tremendous amount of data and the data velocity & volumes are growing exponentially and in turn the growth is continually fuelled up by the evolutions in the access technologies and the access devices. Telco’s are facing a huge challenge than ever with regard to capturing, storing, processing, analysing and extracting meaningful data in near real-time.

Telco data includes CDR – Call detail record, Network data and complete customer data and services data. This Data is a source of rich valuable information which will help the Telco’s to understand their customer behaviour, their network performance, comply to legal requirements, product / service reporting, revenue reporting and turn this data source to a catalyst to generate revenue. Telco’s that exploit this data fully can gain a competitive edge in the fiercely competitive Telecom market that are under tremendous pressure for customer market share and revenue market share.

Few challenges that the Telco’s are facing today in this arena of handling and managing the data are:

  • Multiple Data Sources
  • Data Loading Speed & Time
  • Data Compression
  • Data Storage Costs
  • Complex Data Queries
  • Near Real-time analytics
  • Scalability

We provide solution to Telco’s by addressing these challenges efficiently and effectively to arm them with all the information required to innovate the service offerings in a cost effective manner.

Telco Data & Analytics is a comprehensive modular solution which can be tailored to meet the Telco’s or Service provider’s requirements in capturing, storing, processing, analysing and acting on the service providers Data.

Salient Features & Key-Benefits

Wide range of Data Sources – In any Telecom environment are a mixture of legacy , standard and proprietary data sources and we support any data format from encrypted to legacy to standard to files to csv to connecting to databases directly to receiving direct data triggers with intelligence to enrich this data as required.

Speed – Rapid extraction, cleansing and staging of data in state of the art Data base, which is modelled to suit specific needs which ensures the data is available in as near real-time as possible.

Multi dimension Analysis – Support any of Regulatory Requirement, Customer Analysis, Services Analysis, Network Analysis, Campaign Management, Sales & Financial analysis.

Enhanced Data Compression & reduced storage costs – Data is stored optimally in compressed manner thereby reducing the storage costs.

Simpler Access & Reduced complexity – Intuitive graphical web based user interface with role based access

Scorecard, Dashboards, Email – Create your own Dashboards, Scorecards and reporting formats that will be emailed in the desired format.

Scalable & Cost Effective – Scales out to meet your futuristic requirements of increased data and scope.
