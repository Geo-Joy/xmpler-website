Billing Solutions


  • INTRODUCTION
  • Postpaid Billing
  • Prepaid Bill Generation

Prepaid Subscribers penetration in most of the networks is on much higher side. Number of Postpaid subscribers in a network are normally handful. Also in many networks, operators are using converged rating in the IN for both prepaid and postpaid subscribers.

In both the above scenarios, operators are looking for cost effective and flexible postpaid billing system which can be customized as per their existing environment. Our Billing System allow the creation of different kind of postpaid users and their rating / charging are controlled either by the platform, or leverage the facilities available in the IN. The platform also has an elaborate KYC and Credit Control system, which allows the different UBCC and Credit Watch to be done in order to give the best facilities to the subscribers as well as ensuring that there is no revenue leak.

It also has an elaborate prepaid corporate billing system, which uses the best of prepaid IN and postpaid payment facilities, to give a corporate much more control over the employee numbers.

This allows operators to incorporate Postpaid Subscriber base and various solutions and privileges to be passed onto the Subscriber.

  • Strong KYC to handle Subscriber Profile, including Multi-Language support
  • Profiling done based on Individual, Corporate House, Group Plan etc.
  • Integration with IN (Intelligent Network) to handle Hybrid Subscriber Account
  • Credit Control and Credit Worthiness with Automated Control Manager
  • Risk Manager and Credit Limit Control based on Geographical, Demo-graphical, Re-entrant, Credit history, etc.
  • Multi-tier Bill Generation with Online Review and Re-format
  • Plan Manager, CUG Manager, Dunning Care and Exhaustive Aging Report for Revenue Management
  • Self-Care for Subscriber for Bill Pay and Plan Management along with Predictive Usage Calculator.
  • Option to generate Physical Hard Copies, along with Digital PDF Documents with Password Protection and Dispatch

Option to allow Prepaid Subscriber to be able to generate a Bill of their monthly usage

  • Provision for Subscribers to Self-Register for Detailed Prepaid Bills via Soft copy
  • CDR and EVR manager to handle all call records, posted by the IN for all activities done by Prepaid Subscriber.
  • Online Bill Generation with either Invoice Details or Detailed Call Records, including Various VAS services used by the Prepaid Subscriber
  • Option to generate Physical Hard Copies, along with Digital PDF Documents with Password Protection and Dispatch
  • Option to charge Subscriber on a recurring basis for Services given.
