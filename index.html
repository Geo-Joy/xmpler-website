“We connect businesses with each other, be it content required in a country or a platform required at an operator”. Xmpler is a leading infotainment aggregation company, aggregating premium contents of games, music, movies, sports, health & wealth, bringing information and entertainment to the digital lifestyle of mobile users. In addition to content services aggregation, Xmpler offers content streaming solutions too.



Our mission is to empower and connect users to a variety of rich, personalized and affordable local, regional and global contents.



Our vision is to be the premier content services aggregation company and the premier content streaming solutions company and thereby provide users with contents to enjoy and make informed decisions for a wonderful, healthy & happy life.

Our Team

Shyam Gopinath Menon

Co-Founder & Director

Shyam has over 20 years of Telco experience starting from 1994 when he joined BPL Mobile (now Vodafone India) in Mumbai, then in Kerala. He later joined Wataniya Telecom, Kuwait (Now Ooredoo). Shyam has extensive experience in Sales, Sales Training, Product Development, Product Management, Pricing, Digital Products Management. Shyam is an Engineer from National Institute of Technology, Surat.

Shyam is an avid cricketer, and loves to trek and take amazing pictures of nature and has recently completed treks in the Himalayas, and other parts of India and the Alps.

Justus Samuel George

Co-Founder & Director

Justus has 17 plus years of Telecom Experience primarily in Product Marketing and Product Development functions. He started his career in 1996 with BPL Mobile. He later joined Wataniya Telecom in Kuwait. He held various positions which included Content Products Management and other functions. He was Director for Consumer Product Marketing and later was Director CVM which looked after the Retention and Loyalty functions. Justus has done his MBA from Pune.

Justus is interested in fast machines and likes motorcycles. He has a keen eye to details and loves fonts and type sets.


Abby Elias Chethicattu

Co-Founder & Technology Director

Abby is our Technology Director and has over 18 years of Telecom experience in the Technology function. He started his career in BPL Mobile (now Vodafone India) and then moved onto Wataniya Telecom, Kuwait (now Ooredoo) where he was Director Technology Planning where he looked after Convergent Billing, Circuit Core and Packet Core Modernization, IN implementation and VAS consolidation. He later moved to Zain Kuwait where he was the Convergence planning expert where he looked after IoT and M2M connectivity, BSS transformation and Policy Control for Broadband billing.

Abby is an Engineer from the University of Madras.

Abby is an avid reader of new Technology with his latest interests being in Blockchain.
