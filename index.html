Xmpler is a leading infotainment aggregation company, aggregating premium contents of games, music, movies, sports, health & wealth, bringing information and entertainment to the digital lifestyle of mobile users. In addition to content services aggregation, Xmpler offers content hosting solutions too. Xmpler also connects businesses together by forging business partnerships to offer products as applications and platforms.



Our mission is to empower and connect users to a variety of rich, personalized and affordable local, regional and global contents.



Our vision is to be the premier content services aggregation company and the premier content hosting solutions company and thereby provide users with contents to enjoy and make informed decisions for a wonderful, healthy & happy life.
