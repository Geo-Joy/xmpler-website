Continuous evolutions in access technologies, User device diversifications which are bandwidth hungry, connectivity from any device anywhere, M2M like Health care, Telematics etc., bandwidth hogging contents, OTT applications, Multi-Industry convergence and worldwide adoption of Internet has fuelled the explosive Internet Traffic growth. Mobility, broadband, devices and always on apps have redefined the way people connect and communicate.

Service providers like Mobile, Cable Internet, WiFi, Broadband, Wired, WiMAX are faced with the ever widening gap between the revenues and the cost of supporting this exponential Internet traffic.

Service providers need to innovate and respond to these dynamic market conditions byrapidly offering new, compelling & competitive data services catering to all market segments and protect networks through traffic shaping.

This is achieved by a flexible Access agnostic Unified Policy Control & Charging solution, which enables the service providers to monetize these challenges into revenues by enabling them to Flexibly Rate & Charge data access along with converged Policy Control which delivers customer centric personalised data services.

This results in Efficient utilization of network resources and Service differentiation in the dynamic competitive market. UPCC is based on well-defined 3GPP Standards with Open, Simple interfaces and Modular architecture which is Intelligent and Robust.

UPCC platform offers a fully functional 3GPP compliant Policy Control and Charging Function (PCRF) which enables intelligent policy control decisions and flow based charging control functionalities. PCRF provides network control regarding the service data flow detection, gating, QoS and flow based charging. The PCRF interfaces with packet gateway and applies policy & charging enforcement decisions.

PCRF can be deployed in Networks compliant to 3GPP Release 7, 8, 9 & 11 like 2.5G, 3G, HSPA, LTE, SAE, CDMA - EVDO, IMS, WiMAX and supports 3GPP standard Diameter (Gx, Rx, Sp & Gxx) interfaces with the PCEF, AF, SPR and BBERF respectively.

It also supports Subscriber Profile Repository (SPR) and Online Charging System (OCS), enabling a wide range of sophisticated charging & subscriber rules which enhances the customer experience, usage management & control which are key to service acceptance and customer satisfaction.

UPCC platform offers a Real-time rating and charging centric solution with an integrated 3GPP compliant Subscriber Profile Repository (SPR) & Online Charging System (OCS) which performs online rating, correlation and charging of subscribers accounts / balances for Data access.

Real time data billing solution can be deployed in Networks compliant to 3GPP Release 7, 8, 9 & 11 like 2.5G, 3G, HSPA, LTE, IMS and supports 3GPP standard Gx / Ro interface with the GGSN, P-GW, WLAN, IMS Gateway Function (IMS GWF) or any other standards compliant DPI platforms.

Integration of PCRF & Real Time Data Billing solution offers a plethora of parameters for creation of exhaustive use cases and innovative data packs

UPCC platform offers a truly centralised converged Policy and Charging Enforcement Function (PCEF) that delivers the functionalities of Deep packet Inspection (DPI) / Traffic Detection Function (TDF), traffic shaping, packet filtering & QoS controls, & enforce policy decisions in real-time based on the context of network, service, subscriber, and usage context.

PCEF is access-agnostic and can be deployed across Cable Internet, WiFi, ISP, Broadband, WiMAX, 3G, HSPA, LTE etc.

PCEF, an amalgamation of DPI, Policy & Online Charging, results in subscriber, application & Network aware charging models that helps in meeting regulatory requirements, and manage, maximize the network utilization by bandwidth management, QoS, Gating & prioritisation.
