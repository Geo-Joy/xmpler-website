Content Management & Delivery


  • INTRODUCTION
  • Content Management & Delivery

Mobile devices have increasingly become ubiquitous & sophisticated with different varieties of devices with varying resolution, screen size & multimedia device capabilities. Newer devices are all rich with multimedia features and increasingly connected to the mobile Internet. These devices are driven by a variety of device Operating systems like the Android, iOS, Windows Mobile, Symbian, Black Berry etc., adding even further dimensions to the already existing complexities. Service distinction can only be brought by delivering the digital content & services rapidly in an optimised, tailormade (to the device) & personalised (to the user) way.

Ourversatile Content Management & Delivery platform, is an end-to-end complete ecosystem for rapid provisioning, integration, deployment, and billing of value added services (digital contents) addressing the needs of the major stake holders in the ecosystem - vizmobile subscriber, Mobile Operator / Service Provider and Content Providers. That is configurable and maintainable by the Mobile Operator / Service Provider through a user-friendly wed based graphical user interface.

Content Management & Delivery platform integrates the Content Providers (CP) with the Operators, supporting and managing diversity of partnerships, contents and content related aspects with an innovative and intelligent content management engine.

The platform is also focused on providing the end-users / mobile subscriber, with a simple, easy to use interface to the various content/services through a Rich portal management engine and allowing multiple network interfaces for subscriber interaction like, SMS, USSD etc.

With this end-to-end approach in mind, the system promotes and recommends contents through the uniform and simple to use Portal and network interface (SMS, USSD etc). The delivery of the content-based VAS is agnostic to the network and device capability. This results in driving revenue growth.

Salient Features & Key-Benefits

  • Multi device access

  • Dynamic portal & content rendering
